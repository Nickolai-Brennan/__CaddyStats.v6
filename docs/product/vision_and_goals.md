# Vision and Goals

## Purpose

Define the product-level vision, mission, goals, and success criteria that govern Caddy Stats.

## Vision

Build the most trusted golf analytics and betting intelligence platform by combining transparent data systems, explainable models, and scalable editorial execution.

## Mission

Turn fragmented golf research into a unified, auditable, and monetizable product ecosystem for bettors, fantasy users, analysts, editors, and operators.

## Strategic promise

Caddy Stats should help users move from discovery to deeper decision support without forcing them to trust opaque rankings, generic picks, or unsupported editorial claims.

## Product goals

### User value goals

- deliver reliable player, course, and tournament intelligence
- make model output understandable and reviewable
- support fast weekly research workflows for betting and fantasy preparation
- keep public and premium experiences readable, useful, and grounded in data

### Platform goals

- preserve PostgreSQL as the source of truth
- maintain clean boundaries across stats, content, auth, billing, and audit concerns
- stabilize API contracts before tightly coupling UI experiences to unstable implementation details
- keep AI outputs grounded, reviewable, and logged

### Business goals

- grow qualified organic traffic
- convert anonymous visitors into members and subscribers
- increase recurring subscription revenue
- expand affiliate opportunities tied to verified decision support

## Non-negotiable principles

1. data first
2. explainability over hype
3. architecture before implementation sprawl
4. premium value must be tangible
5. security and governance are part of the product
6. editorial speed cannot come at the cost of trust

## Success measures

Caddy Stats is succeeding when:

- public content attracts qualified golf research traffic
- premium workflows create repeat weekly usage
- editors and analysts can trace claims back to governed data or model sources
- AI-assisted workflows improve velocity without weakening auditability
- monetization grows through differentiated value rather than unsupported promotion
