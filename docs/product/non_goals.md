# Non-Goals

## Current non-goals

The current Caddy Stats foundation does not aim to deliver:

- multi-sport coverage before golf workflows are stable
- native mobile applications as a first-order delivery target
- autonomous sportsbook bet placement or wagering execution
- unsupported real-time claims when providers are delayed or missing
- community or social-network features as part of the initial product core
- AI-generated statistics, injuries, field changes, odds, or confidence claims presented as facts
- premium gating that hides all useful free discovery value

## Why these are out of scope

Each non-goal protects the platform's early focus on trustworthy golf workflows, durable data systems, and a clear value ladder. Expanding into adjacent product categories too early would dilute the architecture, the editorial model, and the monetization signal.

## Revisit conditions

A current non-goal should only be reconsidered when:

- golf-specific workflows are stable and measurable
- upstream data quality and governance are proven
- the capability has a documented architectural fit
- the feature strengthens retention, monetization, or operational leverage
