# Value Proposition

## Core promise

Caddy Stats helps users make smarter golf decisions with data they can inspect and workflows they can repeat.

## What users get

- **One research surface** for player, tournament, course, and market analysis.
- **Explainable projections** instead of black-box rankings.
- **Grounded editorial content** tied to verified or derived data.
- **Premium workflow acceleration** through filters, comparisons, and saved research patterns.
- **Operational trust** through auditability across AI, publishing, and entitlement changes.

## Why it is differentiated

Unlike generic pick sites or disconnected stat tools, Caddy Stats joins structured golf data, model outputs, editorial context, and premium gating inside one governed system.

## Value by audience

- Bettors get faster shortlist creation and clearer reasoning.
- DFS users get richer comparison context and scenario analysis.
- Research-oriented readers get readable content backed by real numbers.
- Editors and analysts get structured workflows that increase speed without lowering rigor.

## Monetization implication

The product's value proposition depends on real utility, not scarcity theater. Premium access should unlock deeper workflow power, not hide the basics needed to establish trust.
