# Product Brief

## One-line summary

Caddy Stats is a data-first golf analytics platform that combines projections, betting intelligence, editorial publishing, and premium research workflows into one governed product.

## Problem space

Golf research is fragmented across sportsbook apps, spreadsheets, stat vendors, and shallow editorial content. Users need one place to evaluate players, tournaments, courses, and betting angles with transparent data support.

## Product promise

Deliver decision-ready golf intelligence that users can trust because the numbers, editorial claims, and AI-assisted outputs all remain grounded, explainable, and auditable.

## Who the product serves

- serious golf bettors
- DFS and fantasy golf players
- research-oriented golf fans
- internal editors, analysts, and admins

## Key capabilities

- player, course, tournament, and market analytics
- projections and model-performance views
- premium research workflows and gating
- structured editorial CMS and SEO publishing
- AI-assisted content and analysis with review controls
- admin and governance tooling for permissions, billing, and auditability

## Monetization model

- free discovery through SEO and public research pages
- account creation and membership for activation flows
- premium subscriptions for advanced workflows and deeper projections
- affiliate value tied to trusted decision support

## Success criteria

- differentiated weekly utility for subscribers
- scalable public traffic from data-backed content
- explainable premium value that converts and retains
- editorial velocity without trust erosion
- auditable internal operations across content, AI, and billing
